Battleship

Game Rules (Click to Open)

Each player has a 10×10 grid labeled A–J and 1–10. Players place ships on their own grid.

Players take turns calling out a grid location (example: A7) over the radio.

The opponent replies with “Hit” or “Miss”.

Mark your shots on the opponent grid. Mark incoming shots on your own grid.

Keyboard Entry:
Type A7 /hit or B3 /miss


Drag your ships onto your grid:
Battleship (4)
Cruiser (3)
Destroyer (2)
